Core capabilities

Specialist insight across the asset lifecycle.

Integrated inspection, assessment, and reliability services designed around the condition and criticality of your assets.

Pipeline integrity

Understand condition. Control risk. Extend service life.

Data-driven integrity assessment for onshore and offshore pipeline systems, covering corrosion, defects, inspection planning, and intervention priorities.

  • In-line inspection data review
  • Corrosion growth assessment
  • Integrity management planning
Risk-based inspection

Focus inspection resources where they create the most value.

Structured RBI programmes that connect probability, consequence, degradation mechanisms, and inspection effectiveness.

  • API 580 / API 581 aligned studies
  • Damage mechanism review
  • Optimised inspection intervals
Fitness for service & RLA

Make confident run, repair, or replace decisions.

Engineering assessments for equipment affected by thinning, cracking, dents, distortion, and other service-related damage.

  • API 579 Level 1, 2, and 3 assessment
  • Remaining life calculations
  • Safe operating envelope definition
Asset integrity management

Connect people, data, and decisions in one integrity framework.

Lifecycle integrity systems that align inspection, maintenance, risk, compliance, and performance information.

  • AIM framework development
  • Integrity KPI and governance setup
  • Data quality and gap assessment
Rotating equipment

Protect the machinery at the heart of production.

Condition and reliability support for turbines, compressors, pumps, and other dynamic equipment.

  • Vibration and condition analysis
  • Root cause failure analysis
  • Reliability improvement strategy
